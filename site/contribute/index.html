Contribute

There are many ways to contribute to this initiative: Please communicate with us on the mailing lists, send us pull requests for any of the subproject, or just tell us your success story adopting freesmartphone.org.

Mailing list

Our mailing list is fso at openphoenux.org, gracefully hosted by the guys from the OpenPhoenux project. You can join and/or find the new (post 2014) archives at lists.openphoenux.org.

I have a dump of the old archives, which I’m going to upload soon.

Source code

The source code for this site and all of the FSO subprojects is at github/freesmartphone. We have an organization account – feel free to send us your username, if you want to join.

IRC

A life chat is on freenode.net:6667 – for now we are (ab)using the #openmoko-cdevel channel. Depending on the project focus and the amount of traffic, we may also use #freesmartphone.org in the future.
